Our Training Programs

Freedom Reign Basketball Academy offers structured programs for players at every level. From beginners to elite athletes, we nurture potential and build future stars.

Beginner Academy (Ages 7–12)

Fundamental skills training in dribbling, shooting, passing, and teamwork. Perfect for kids just starting their basketball journey.

Youth Development (Ages 13–17)

Focused training for young athletes with game strategy, strength building, and team play. Designed to prepare for competitive levels.

Elite Performance (Ages 17+)

Advanced program for serious players aiming for college or professional basketball. Includes personal coaching and video review.

Weekend Camps & Tryouts

Open seasonal sessions for new players, talent discovery, and skill sharpening. Join to experience the Freedom Reign difference.
